Interviewer

Nathan Wong
Student of Monta Vista High School (Generation Z)

Who is he?
Nathan Wong is currently a Junior at Monta Vista High School (Class of 2017). In terms of American history, Nathan is interested in leadership figures of the past as well as the grassroots movements which define American society. The interconnected nature of history is by virtue comprised of multitudes of different perspectives, all of which form the American narrative as we know and study. 

Interviewees

Mr. Scott DeRuiter
Generation X

Who is he?
Mr. DeRuiter is a Monta Vista High School AP Calculus BC and AP Computer Science teacher. He has taught for over 20 years, after having pursued a degree in math at Stanford University. Born in a rural city in California, Mr. DeRuiter found many practical applications of math throughout his experiences.

﻿Ms. Mia Onodera
Generation Y

﻿Who is she?
Ms. Onodera is a Monta Vista High School Chemistry teacher. She has taught for over 20 years, after having acquired industry experience at various chemistry-related companies. Ms. Onodera is very passionate about her teaching and her experiences in transitioning from a young teacher speak translucently to generational and cultural differences today.

﻿Mrs. Irene Wong
Baby Boom B

Who is she?
Mrs. Irene Wong is a stay-at-home mother of 2 kids. Having immigrated to the United States when she was just 11 years old, Mrs. Wong faced the difficulties of a first-generation Chinese American. Mrs. Wong takes pride in her Chinese heritage and hopes that her kids will someday understand the interaction between American and Chinese culture.

​Mr. Albert Boledo
Silent Generation A

Who is he?
Mr. Albert Boledo is a senior resident of Cupertino. Currently retired with his wife, Mr. Boledo’s parents immigrated to the United States and he has made this place his permanent residence ever since. Mr. Boledo has seen much of American history – the ups and down – and has much to say about the growing reliance on technology.

﻿Mr. Preston Wong
Millennial

Who is he?
Mr. Preston Wong is a college student at Purdue University. Intending to major in pharmacy, he is very passionate about chemistry and how it can be used to save many people around the world. Mr. Wong grew up in an age of technological expansion and has very insightful opinions on the growing interconnected data networks.

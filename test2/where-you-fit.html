About this tool.

This tool was made so that interested individuals can submit their own information, such as age and key events in their lifetime, so that the information can be indexed in a simple database and can thus relay key incidents people around their generation would also most likely experience and the sentiments that are associated with that event. Since all events and sentiments are currently manually entered, turnaround times will lag.

*Please note that results may not be fully accurate or reflective of the generation as a whole. Use at your discretion.

﻿﻿Generation Defining Moment 

﻿A generation is defined by a loose collection of cultural motifs, ranging from shared tragedies to collective appreciation of art and technology. Each generation has its own unique experiences, events which history will inscribe forever in the passage of time. These moments contain an array of expression – emotions which unify America and invigorate the spirit of patriotism.
